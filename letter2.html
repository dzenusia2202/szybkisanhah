this is for you

i don’t really know where to begin, because every beginning feels too small for what i want to say. so i’ll just start here: this page exists because of you.

you probably don’t realize how much space you take in my thoughts, how often i catch myself wanting to tell you something small, or just sit quietly with you and feel calm for a moment.

being around you feels warm in a way that’s hard to explain. not loud, not overwhelming — just safe.

i admire you more than i usually admit. the way you think, the way you speak, the way you are yourself without trying too hard.

i made this little place for you because you matter to me.

and there’s one more thing… if you’re ready.
